## Positioning
- **One line:** "Your life admin, finally handled."
- **Who:** busy people drowning in receipts, bookings, school letters, renewals —
  families, students, young people. Calm, private, does the boring bits for you.
- **Why now:** AI that actually reads your stuff and turns it into tasks, a
  calendar and a searchable vault — with wellbeing built in.

## Assets we have (given by Arjun)
1. **Billboards at Hull KR (Hull Kingston Rovers) home ground.** Big local OOH to
   a captive, family-heavy East-Yorkshire crowd on match days.
2. **A connection to the founder of Galaxy Pizza (India).** A route to the India
   market, a cross-promo partner, and a possible advisor/intro.

## Channel 1 — Hull KR billboards
A billboard has ~3 seconds. It needs: a huge benefit line, the brand, and ONE
easy action (a short URL + a QR code).
- **Creative:** "Life admin, handled." + "Snap a receipt. We sort the rest." +
  logo + QR + short URL.
- **Action:** QR → a campaign landing that explains the offer and sends people to
  sign up, with the visit attributed so we can measure it.
- **Offer:** a launch perk, e.g. "Scan for 3 months of Plus, free." (Decide the
  offer — see promo codes below.)
- **Measure:** a campaign code / link so every Hull KR signup is countable.

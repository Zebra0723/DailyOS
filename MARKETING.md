## Positioning
- **One line:** "Your life admin, finally handled."
- **Who:** busy people drowning in receipts, bookings, school letters, renewals —
  families, students, young people. Calm, private, does the boring bits for you.
- **Why now:** AI that actually reads your stuff and turns it into tasks, a
  calendar and a searchable vault — with wellbeing built in.

   what works there and apply the *playbook* to DailyOS.

## Channel 1 — Hull KR billboards
A billboard has ~3 seconds. It needs: a huge benefit line, the brand, and ONE
easy action (a short URL + a QR code).
- **Creative:** "Life admin, handled." + "Snap a receipt. We sort the rest." +
  logo + QR + short URL.
- **Action:** QR → a campaign landing that explains the offer and sends people to
  sign up, with the visit attributed so we can measure it.
- **Offer:** a launch perk, e.g. "Scan for 3 months of Plus, free." (Decide the
  offer — see promo codes below.)
- **Measure:** a campaign code / link so every Hull KR signup is countable.

## The Galaxy Pizza playbook (skills we can borrow)
What we learned studying their marketing, and how it transfers to DailyOS:
- **Occasion-led social content.** They hang campaigns on moments (Valentine's,
  festivals, seasons). → DailyOS: build a content calendar around *life-admin
  moments* — tax deadlines, back-to-school, renewal season, New-Year "get
  organised". Same idea, our subject.
- **Win the marketplace where people already are.** They live on Zomato & Swiggy
  and compete on ratings, offers and fast delivery. → DailyOS: win the equivalent
  surfaces — app directories / PWA discoverability, reviews/ratings, and a strong
  launch offer. Show up where intent already exists.
- **Hyperlocal, outlet-by-outlet growth.** They expand location by location rather
  than boiling the ocean. → DailyOS: launch community by community — start with
  Hull (the billboard), nail it, then repeat the playbook elsewhere.
- **Offer-led acquisition.** Combos and limited-time deals drive trial. → DailyOS:
  clear launch offers + bundles (Plus/Pro), funnelling into the referral ladder.
- **Reviews + word-of-mouth as an engine.** Ratings drive discovery for them. →
  DailyOS: prompt happy users for reviews, and lean on the referral ladder for
  word-of-mouth.
- **Use the founder directly:** a content-calendar review, offer/creative feedback,
  and a hyperlocal-launch checklist from someone who's done it. 🔶 Arjun to set up.

## Owned growth (already built — just push it)
- **Referral ladder:** friends both get 10%; refer more → 3mo Plus → 1yr Pro →
  lifetime. This is the engine — every billboard scan should drop people into a
  referral-friendly flow so they invite others.
- **PWA install:** "Add to Home Screen" makes it feel like an app, no store needed.
